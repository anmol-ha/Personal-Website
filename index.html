Hey there! My name is Anmol. I am a journalist and social media editor from India, specialising in gender, health, wellness, social justice, food, and culture. My work has been featured in The BMJ, The Christian Science Monitor, Xtra Magazine, Whetstone SA, Ozy, Autostraddle, Vogue India, Article 14, Mint Lounge, Deccan Herald, and others. In addition, I offer content writing and social media services (strategy, content creation, etc.) to clients working in media, mental health, and wellness spaces.

I also write poetry, reviews, and creative non-fiction. I've recently started dabbling in coding as well with ongoing projects using Python, Flask, HTML-CSS, and JavaScript.

I am currently working as the Social Media & Audience Engagement Lead at queerbeat, an independent online magazine covering LGBTQIA+ persons, in their own voice, in India.
